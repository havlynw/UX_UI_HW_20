Introducing OM!

An app that helps sooth the overwhelmed and anxious through the use of distractions with a purpose; ‘OM’ helps users remain grounded when faced with environments that may be overstimulating or episodes of anxiety.

Lo-Fi

These are our initial ideas for OM!

We wanted an application that was welcoming to Alfie, and and application that provided Alfie with resources that could assist them in providing relief; the information we gathered after performing desk research and following our user interviews suggested that users most wanted healthy distractions and activities while experiencing an episode.

As a result - our teams designed Lo-Fi digital sketches - and we better implemented grounding-work tools such as breathwork and the 54321 technique, along with distractions such as our Bubble Pop and Paint Mixing for our fidgeting features!

Mid-Fi

After conducting out intial testings with our Lo-Fi, we discovered that although a significant portion of our users understood the functions of OM, individuals just begining to understand anxiety and its powerful effects, struggled in determining some of OM's grounding excercises worked; leaving users lost and frustrated.

We realized that bridging this gap in our data was a top priority for OM! Being 'designed with purpose' meant that OM needed to be reliable, affordable, and inclusive.
